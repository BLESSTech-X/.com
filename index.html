About BLESSTech-X

Empowering Zambian Businesses with Digital Solutions

Founded by Barack Blessings in Lusaka, BLESSTech-X is dedicated to helping small and medium-sized Zambian businesses thrive in the digital age. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities in the Zambian market.

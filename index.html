About BLESSTech-X

Empowering Zambian Businesses with Digital & E-commerce Solutions

Founded by Barack Blessings & Sylvester Mwila in Lusaka, BLESSTech-X is dedicated to helping small and medium-sized Zambian businesses thrive in the digital age. We specialize in creating online stores that work for the Zambian market.

Our mission is simple: to make professional e-commerce services accessible and affordable for every entrepreneur. We believe that every business, no matter how small, deserves a strong online presence to sell their products.
